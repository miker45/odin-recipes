Chicken Garlic Pizza

A tasty choice for homemade pizza! Butter garlic sauce, chicken, tomato, and ricotta and parmesan cheeses are baked together for a delightfully light pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 skinless, boneless chicken breast half
  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust dough
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese

  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Place the chicken breast in a saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, and cook until no longer pink, about 20 minutes. Drain and cool slightly, then cut into strips.
  2. Meanwhile, in a small skillet over medium heat, melt butter with garlic, onion and basil. Pour into a chilled dish to cool, and refrigerate until set.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Roll out the pizza sough, place onto a pizza pan or other baking sheet, and spread the herb butter over the surface using th e back of a spoon. Arrange chicken on top, then dot with ricotta cheese. Top with tomato slice, cilantro and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the p;reheated oven, until crust is browned and center is cooked through.
