Old Fashioned Pancakes

My grandmothers favorite pancake recipe, just like she used to make them.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or coop the batter onto the griddle , using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both side and serve hot.
